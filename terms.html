Participating Terms

These Participation Terms include the Brussels Munch terms & conditions and code of conduct. They have been presented at the FetLife event page and on our website. Participants must check in with the Brussels Munch team (BM team) upon arrival and wear the provided participant sticker during the event. By attending and wearing the sticker, you confirm that you have read, understood and agreed to these participation terms. If you do not agree, please do not attend.

The sticker must be visible as it helps identify participants to the BM team, other participants and venue staff. You may choose what name, nickname or pronouns you wish to put on the sticker; we do not require your real name.

Behaviour

Everyone attending the Brussels Munch is expected to behave respectfully towards others during the event, in connection with the event, and in communications linked to the munch. For example, sending messages to several attendees you do not know, before the event, is an example of unwanted behaviour.

Brussels Munch is a social gathering only and not a platform for unwanted approaches, recruitment to events or promotion for your business. Participants are expected to respect the privacy and confidentiality of others. Photography, filming or recording is not allowed during the event. Sharing any information about a participant requires their consent.

The event is in a vanilla public setting and your behaviour and attire should reflect this. Collars (e.g. jewelry with a hook to fasten a leash), clothing with provocative wording or fetish gear should be left at home.

Safety and BM team decisions

The BM team take reasonable steps to support a safe and respectful environment but cannot guarantee the behaviour of participants. Participants remain personally responsible for their own actions and interactions. Attending the munch is voluntary and at your own risk and responsibility. Attendance is only open to people aged 18 or older.

The blue star logo with the yellow circle behind it belongs to the Brussels Munch and its main organizer(s) and shall not be used for any other purpose but for the main Brussels Munch and its related Newbies Munch.

The BM team reserves the right to refuse entry, ask someone to leave, or deny future participation where behaviour is considered incompatible with the safety, wellbeing or spirit of the event. Where privacy or safety concerns are involved, the BM team may limit the level of detail provided about such decisions.
