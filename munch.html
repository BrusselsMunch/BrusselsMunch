A Munch is an informal gathering around BDSM and related kinks that offers, in a “vanilla” setting, the opportunity to meet other people who experience BDSM to varying degrees or are simply interested or curious.

The Brussels Munch takes place in the center of this multicultural city. You will therefore often hear English, French, Dutch and sometimes even other languages being spoken. Whichever of these languages you speak, you will always find someone to talk to.

The word “Munch” comes from the English verb “to munch” and finds its origin in the first BDSM gatherings organized in restaurants. During the discussions at the Brussels Munch, you can therefore also enjoy a snack and/or a drink. Participation in a Munch is entirely free; you only pay for what you eat or drink.

Each munch is supervised by at least one member of the Brussels Munch team, to whom you can always turn. Team members are easily recognizable by their badge with the Brussels Munch logo.

The Brussels Munch meets on the last Tuesday of every month (except December 3) in a bar in the center of Brussels, easily accessible by car and public transport. We meet from 6:30 PM to 10:00 PM.

In order to keep our munches safe and pleasant for everyone, our guests must accept our conditions of participation. The location of the munch is communicated after making contact so that we can confirm that you have read and accepted these terms.
