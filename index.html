Participation Foundations

Strict rules maintained dynamically across all spaces.

01

Vanilla Setting

We meet in an ordinary public venue. You must wear regular street attire. No explicit BDSM play or tools are permitted.

02

Privacy & Ethics

Photography or digital logging is completely prohibited. Respect anonymity and never message attendees unexpectedly prior to gatherings.

03

Age Limit

Absolute strict attendance baseline threshold for ages 18+. Proper age verification can be requested by the hosts.

Deelnamevoorwaarden
